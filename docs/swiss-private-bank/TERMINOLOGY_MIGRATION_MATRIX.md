## Banned language guidance
Avoid in product and marketing copy:
- `disrupt`
- `revolutionary`
- `game changing`
- `AI-powered`
- `next-gen`
- `supercharge`
- `unlock`

Preferred vocabulary:
- `record`
- `continuity`
- `custody`
- `integrity`
- `trail`
- `supersession`
- `provenance`
- `mandate`
- `dossier`
