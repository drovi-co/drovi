## 1) Strategic Translation: Product Plan -> Market Narrative

### 1.1 Category Definition
Drovi should position World Brain as:
- **Institutional Cognition Infrastructure (ICI)**
- Not a productivity assistant
- Not a note-taking memory graph
- Not alerting middleware

Core framing:
- Existing systems store records and summarize text.
- World Brain continuously computes what is true, what is changing, and what action is governed.

### 1.2 Buyer Pain (Founder-Grade)
Target pain statements to repeat across landing + deck:
- "Our reality changes faster than our decisions."
- "We cannot prove what we believed when decisions were made."
- "External shocks hit before our internal systems register impact."
- "AI outputs are fast but not accountable."

### 1.3 Value Pillars (Translate Technical Depth Into Buyer Outcomes)
1. **Live World Twin**
- Combines internal operations + external reality
- Continuously updates exposure topology

2. **Evidence-Backed Belief System**
- Epistemic state for every important claim
- Full provenance and contradiction handling

3. **Causal + Counterfactual Intelligence**
- Understands likely impact paths
- Compares intervention options before action

4. **Normative + Policy Guardrails**
- Encodes law/policy/contract constraints
- Flags pre-breach conditions early

5. **Temporal Accountability**
- Bi-temporal memory of belief and validity windows
- Supports audits and post-incident reconstruction

### 1.4 Founder Narrative Arc
Use this arc in both artifacts:
1. **Why now**: institutions are overloaded by signal velocity and autonomous agents.
2. **What breaks**: summaries and static systems cannot maintain coherent institutional truth.
3. **What we built**: a live cognitive substrate with evidence, causality, and governed interventions.
4. **Why we win**: deep systems architecture + modelops + policy-first operational design.
5. **Why this matters**: fewer unseen shocks, faster incident clarity, lower liability, higher strategic response speed.

### 1.5 Proof-Oriented Claims (Use as Targets, Not Guaranteed Marketing Numbers)
Use these as directional outcomes from the world-brain plan:
- Up to **10x reduction** in unseen high-severity external impact events
- Up to **5x faster** root-cause reconstruction
- **>90% evidence coverage** on high-stakes beliefs
- Measurable decline in contradiction recurrence
- ROI via prevented liability and avoided opportunity decay
